Child Safety Standards

Child Safety · TailTopia

TailTopia is a social app for pet lovers where people share posts, comments, and photos of their pets. Keeping our community safe — and protecting minors in particular — is a responsibility we take with the utmost seriousness. This page describes our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Zero tolerance for CSAE

TailTopia has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content that sexualizes, endangers, or exploits minors. Such content and behavior are strictly prohibited anywhere on the platform. There are no exceptions.

Minimum age

TailTopia is intended for users aged 16 and older. The platform is not directed to children, and accounts found to belong to underage users may be removed.

Content moderation

We combine multiple layers of moderation to detect and remove harmful content:

  • In-app reporting: every post and comment can be reported by any user, so the community can flag content that violates our rules.
  • Automated detection: we use automated systems to help identify potentially harmful or abusive content for review.
  • Human review: reported and flagged content is reviewed by our moderation team, which makes the final decision and takes action.

How we handle violations

When we identify CSAE or related violations, we act promptly to:

  • Remove the content from the platform.
  • Suspend or permanently ban the accounts involved.
  • Report to the relevant authorities and child-protection organizations (such as the NCMEC and competent law-enforcement agencies) as required by law, and preserve evidence to support investigations.

How to report inappropriate content

If you come across content that may exploit or endanger a minor, please report it immediately:

  • Open the post or comment in the app.
  • Tap the ⋯ (more) menu → Report, then choose the appropriate reason.

You can also reach a real person, or escalate an urgent concern, by emailing cs@tailtopia.id. If a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services first.

Legal compliance

TailTopia complies with applicable child-safety laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where it operates, and cooperates with law enforcement and child-protection authorities. We review and update these standards as our product and the applicable legal requirements evolve.

Contact our Child Safety team Questions or reports regarding child safety can be sent to cs@tailtopia.id.
