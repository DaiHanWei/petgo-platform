Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

TailTopia uses Sign in with Google. Open the app and tap the sign-in button, then choose your Google account. No separate password is required.

How do I create a pet profile?

After signing in, go to Profile → Add Pet and fill in your pet's details. You can add a photo, name, species, and birthday.

How do I reset or recover my account?

Because TailTopia signs in through Google, account access is managed by your Google account. If you can sign in to Google, you can sign in to TailTopia. For other access issues, email us at cs@tailtopia.id.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app and go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or see our account deletion page for details. You may also request deletion by email.

How is my data handled?

Please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for how we collect, use, and protect your information.

How do I report a problem or abuse?

Email cs@tailtopia.id with the details and we will look into it promptly.
