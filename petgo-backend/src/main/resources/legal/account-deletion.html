Delete Your TailTopia Account

Account Deletion · TailTopia

Delete in the app

Open the app and go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. After you confirm, your account is deleted immediately.

Request by email

You can also request deletion by sending an email to cs@tailtopia.id with the subject "Account Deletion Request". Please include the email address associated with your TailTopia account so we can verify and locate it. Email requests are processed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove:

  • Profile information — name, photo, and bio
  • Your posts and comments
  • Your pet profiles
  • Your email address and authentication data

Data retention

Some data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 90 days after deletion, after which it is fully purged. We may retain certain information for longer where required to meet legal or regulatory obligations.

Need help? Questions about deleting your account? Contact us at cs@tailtopia.id.
